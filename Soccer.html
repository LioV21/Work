Soccer

The sport that brings together millions of fans and players across the globe.

Soccer originated over 2,000 years ago in ancient civilizations like China, Greece, and Rome.
The rules of modern soccer were created in 1863 in England, which is why it's often called "The Beautiful Game."
Soccer is the world's most popular sport with over 4 billion fans worldwide.
The FIFA World Cup is the largest international soccer tournament, first held in 1930.
